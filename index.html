Small-Scale Multi-Unit Housing

The new shape of the single-family lot.

British Columbia's SSMUH legislation rewrote what's possible on standard residential lots — across every municipality in Metro Vancouver. Dawar leads the missing-middle side of the practice, working with homeowners from West Vancouver to Surrey to model whether co-development, duplex conversion, or a small multiplex makes sense on their specific lot.

01 · Site Analysis

What your lot can actually do.

Every lot has a different ceiling under SSMUH — depending on the municipality's bylaws, the lot size, the servicing, and the existing structure. We pull the numbers before you commit to anything.

02 · Financial Modelling

The math, before the architect.

Build cost, financing structure, sale or rental projections, holding strategy. We walk through whether the upside justifies the disruption — for your family, your timeline, and your tax position.

03 · The Right Team

Architect, builder, lender, lawyer.

SSMUH projects fail when one piece of the team is wrong. We've built relationships with architects, builders, lenders, and planning consultants across Metro Vancouver who do this work properly.
