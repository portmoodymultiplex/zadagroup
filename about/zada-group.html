About Us

A boutique real estate team built around the people we serve.

Family-first, community-driven, and a little obsessed with doing it well.

Zada Group is a boutique real estate team operating across Metro Vancouver, licensed with Stonehaus Realty Corp. Our team is led by Dawar Zada, PREC*, a Master Medallion Club REALTOR®, and we are deliberately built to be different in a few specific ways.

A diverse team is the point.

We believe a real estate team is most useful to clients when the people on it have lived different lives. Our agents bring different first languages, different specialties, and different experiences of what buying or selling a home actually feels like. Randy has built a practice around clients in law enforcement, government, and the military, and speaks Vietnamese. Liz has international experience from years working in Mexico, lives in New Westminster, and has become the agent families call when seniors and estates are involved. Dawar leads the team's work in missing-middle development and large-scale strategy. Between us, we cover a lot of ground, and that range turns out to matter a lot.

Between the team we speak English, French, Vietnamese, and Farsi. If a client is more comfortable in any of those, we can take the whole conversation there.

Community is not a marketing word for us.

We host community events because we want to be in this neighbourhood for a long time. A few of the ones we've put on:

  • A community carnival open to anyone who wanted to come.
  • Photos with Santa during the holidays.
  • Education seminars on SSMUH, the Port Moody multiplex changes, and what they mean for residents.
  • Private movie screenings, just for our clients, because it's a nice way to say thank you.

The team behind the team.

Two people don't usually get put on a website, but they should. Without them, Zada Group is not Zada Group.

Claudine Dufresne

Claudine is the powerhouse behind the entire operation. Fluent in English and French, she came to Zada Group with years of experience working inside brokerages in Montreal and across Canada, and she brought a structural rigor to our backend that is, and we don't say this lightly, unmatched. If a file moves through Zada Group cleanly, it's because of Claudine. If a client gets the right document at the right time without anyone asking twice, it's because of Claudine. The team runs because she runs it.

Sheri Zada

Sheri is the brain behind Zada Group's marketing and community relations, and she's also Dawar's wife. Her background is in communications, but her real interest is in the intersection of real estate and local government. She works directly with municipal politicians, federal representatives, and city planning departments to help them understand the impact of real estate decisions on the communities they oversee, and to help Zada Group's building clients work through that world from the other side.

She is also the person who built this website. The coder credit is hers. When she's not at a planning meeting or in front of a screen, she's probably playing with her three kids, baking, or in the car listening to music at a volume that everyone in the family has opinions about.

What we stand for.

Our brand promise is short and we try to actually live it: guiding you home with integrity, innovation, and care, every time. That means honest advice when the deal is bad. It means staying creative when the standard path doesn't work. And it means treating clients the way we'd want our own families treated, because most of the time, they are.
