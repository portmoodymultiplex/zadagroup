About Us

A boutique real estate team built around the people we serve.

Family-first, community-driven, and a little obsessed with doing it well.

Zada Group is a boutique real estate team operating across Metro Vancouver, licensed with Stonehaus Realty Corp. Led by Dawar Zada, PREC*, a Master Medallion Club REALTOR®, working out of Coquitlam, BC.

The team.

Three agents with different specialties. Dawar leads, runs the SSMUH and Port Moody Multiplex practice, and handles investors and developers. Randy is MRP certified, works with law enforcement, government, and military clients, and speaks Vietnamese. Liz is SRES designated and works with seniors and their families through downsizing, estates, and power-of-attorney sales. All three handle first-time buyers through luxury.

Community.

We host community events in Port Moody because we live here. A few we've put on:

  • Community carnival, open to anyone.
  • Photos with Santa.
  • SSMUH and multiplex education seminars.
  • Private movie screenings for clients.

The team behind the team.

Claude Dufresne

Operations lead. Fluent in English and French. Years of brokerage experience in Montreal and across Canada. Claude runs the back end of every transaction. If a file moves through cleanly, it's because of her.

Sheri Zada

Marketing and community relations. Communications background. Works with municipal, provincial, and federal politicians and city planning departments on the impact of real estate decisions on local communities, and helps Zada Group's building clients work through municipal processes.

She built this website. When she's not in front of a screen or at a planning meeting, she's with her three kids, baking, or in the car listening to loud music.

How we work.

Honest advice when the deal is bad. Creative when the standard path doesn't work. Family-first.
