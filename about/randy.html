Randy Quach

Burnaby-born, hockey-raised, and never far from the rink.

Speaks English and Vietnamese. Based in Port Moody. Military Relocation Professional (MRP) certified.

Randy was born and raised in Burnaby and has been playing hockey his whole life. He still plays, and he coaches. The team, the ice time, the kids he coaches: that's the side passion that runs alongside the work.

His real estate practice specializes in law enforcement, government, and military clients. People with schedules that don't bend, privacy that matters, and no patience for sales pitches. He also works with a lot of first-time buyers and with investors who want a frank read on a property.

Randy is MRP (Military Relocation Professional) certified and speaks English and Vietnamese.

He lives in Port Moody with Vivi, his favourite person in the world. The two of them are unofficial foodies in PoMo. Ask him for restaurant recommendations.
