Marketing

Marketing that moves homes.

In real estate, your marketing is your reach. Every listing we bring to Port Moody, the Tri-Cities, and Metro Vancouver gets a strategy designed specifically for the property, the price point, and the buyer pool. This is what that looks like.

Targeted Social Media

Your listing, in front of the right buyers.

Every property we list gets its own social media campaign, designed for the buyer most likely to fall in love with it. We don't run the same ad for a Sapperton character home that we run for a Port Moody multiplex pre-sale. The creative changes. The targeting changes. The platforms change.

And it works. Our last campaign reached 1,622 viewers — 88% of them weren't even our followers yet. That's the difference between marketing to the people who already know you, and marketing to the buyer who's actually going to walk through the door.

Custom Listing Websites

Every luxury listing gets its own home online.

For our high-end listings, a standard MLS® page isn't enough. We build a dedicated single-property website for the home — its own domain, its own cinematic hero, its own custom-typeset name, full photo gallery, neighbourhood story, and a direct path to book a private showing.

Buyers remember the website. They share it. They come back to it. It does work that no social ad, brochure, or MLS listing can do — it turns a property into a destination.

Video & Drone

Cinematic. Aerial. Story-led.

Photos sell a listing online. Video sells the lifestyle. Every property gets professional cinematography and, where it makes sense, drone footage — the kind that lets a buyer fall for the home before they've ever walked through the door. We treat every listing like a short film, because that's increasingly how buyers decide.

Subscribe to our YouTube channel for current listings, market updates, and behind-the-scenes from the team.

Professional Photography

The first impression is everything.

The first thing a buyer sees of your home is a thumbnail on a screen. If that photo doesn't stop them, nothing else gets a chance. Every Zada Group listing is photographed by professional real estate photographers — natural light, wide-angle, edited the way the listing deserves. We don't list a home until the photos are right.

Detailed laser-measured floor plans accompany every set. Buyers can walk your home before they walk your home.

Luxury Feature Brochures

Print that the buyer takes home.

Digital marketing gets the buyer to the showing. The brochure they take home is what keeps the listing top-of-mind. Every Zada Group listing — from a $499K condo to a $5M+ luxury home — gets a custom-designed feature brochure: full-bleed photography, floor plans, neighbourhood story, builder details where relevant.

Printed, hand-finished, and stocked at every open house, sent to every interested buyer, and mailed to surrounding neighbours during the campaign.

For Developers
