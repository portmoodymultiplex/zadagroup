About this home

A rare top-floor opportunity in one of West Coquitlam's best buildings.

Top-floor 2 bed + den at The Oaks by Strand. 925 sqft of bright, well-designed living with an oversized covered balcony, laminate floors, and big windows. Modern kitchen with quartz counters, stainless appliances, and gas range. Flexible den ideal for office or nursery. Spa-inspired ensuite off the primary.

Enjoy 27,000+ sqft of resort-style amenities: fitness studio, co-work space, theatre, games room, library, playground, and BBQ courtyards. Includes 1 parking, 1 storage locker, and 1 bike storage room.

Steps to Burquitlam SkyTrain, minutes to Lougheed Town Centre, SFU, Hwy 1, and Port Moody's shops and restaurants. Walkable to Miller Park Elementary, École Banting Middle, and Port Moody Secondary.
