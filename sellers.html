The Wealth Journey

The sell is half of it.

For Dawar, real estate isn't a singular transaction. It's a wealth-building journey. The buy is just as important as the sell, and they should feel like one continuous move, not two separate deals.

01

Strategy starts before the sign goes up.

We sit down well before the listing date and map the full journey. What does the sale need to net? What does the next home cost? Where are the gaps? The plan accounts for both moves, not just the one we're being paid for today.

02

Pricing built off actual sales, not asking prices.

Comparables, buyer pool depth, what's actively under offer in the neighbourhood, where the market is leaning this month. We bring the numbers to the first meeting and tell you what we think a record-breaking outcome looks like for your specific property.

03

Completion dates that actually line up.

Dawar matches his clients' buys perfectly with their sale completion dates so the journey is seamless. No double mortgages. No surprise short-term rentals. No move-out scrambles. One continuous move, treated like one continuous deal.

04

A team carrying the whole thing.

Randy and Liz have been working closely with Dawar for years. They're not assistants. They're licensed realtors who know how he prices, how he negotiates, and how he closes. While Dawar is leading strategy on your sale, you get two more senior agents quietly making sure nothing in the journey gets dropped.

05

Marketing that actually moves the price up.

MLS is the floor. On top of it: professional photography, broker tours, paid social, our private buyer database, quiet pre-list outreach when the property calls for it. You'll see the marketing plan in writing before we start, with numbers attached.

06

The handoff.

Subject removal, financing confirmation, the buyer's lawyer, the title insurer, the move itself. We run the whole closing for you and stay involved through possession day, because that's the moment the next chapter actually starts.
