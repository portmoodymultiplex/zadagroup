For Sellers

Seller's Guide.

Pricing, prep, marketing, and what actually moves a property in Metro Vancouver.

1. Pricing.

We price off recent comparable sales, not asking prices. Depending on your property and the current buyer pool, we'll either price under to drive offers or price at value to attract serious buyers. We bring the comps to the first meeting.

2. Prep.

Before listing: paint, deep clean, declutter, fix the obvious. Stage if it makes sense for the price point. We have trades and stagers we work with regularly.

3. Photography.

Most buyers see your home online first. We use professional real estate photographers and don't list until the photos are right.

4. Marketing.

MLS is the floor. On top of that: paid social, broker tours, open houses where they make sense, outreach to our buyer database, and quiet listings before the public launch when the property calls for it. You'll see a written marketing plan before we start.

5. Subject removal and closing.

Inspection, financing, buyer's lawyer, title insurer. We manage all of it so the deal closes on schedule.

6. The move.

Completion, possession, moving day. We help with the logistics, not just the paperwork.

Just curious about value?

We do free home valuations. Quiet, no follow-up calls unless you ask.
