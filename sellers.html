For Sellers

The Zada Group Seller's Guide.

What we'd tell a friend who was selling. Honest version.

1. Price is a strategy, not a wish.

The wrong list price costs more than money. It costs market days, and market days cost more money. We price using actual comparable sales (not asking prices), active competition, and the specific shape of the buyer pool right now. There's a version of this where we price under to create a bidding war and a version where we price right at value to capture serious offers without scaring the room. Both can be right. We'll tell you which one is right for you and why.

2. Prep matters more than you think.

The cheapest dollars you spend selling a home are the ones you spend before it's listed. Paint, declutter, deep clean, address the obvious deferred maintenance, professional staging if it makes sense. Almost everyone is glad they did it. Almost no one regrets not doing more.

3. Photography is the listing.

Ninety-something percent of buyers see your home online before they see it in person. If the photos don't pull them in, the showing doesn't happen. We work with professional real estate photographers, and we don't list until the photos are right.

4. Marketing is more than MLS.

MLS gets you on the boards. After that, what actually moves a property is the rest of the plan: targeted social media, neighbourhood outreach to known buyers, our internal client database, broker tours, open houses where they make sense, and (sometimes) deliberate quiet listings before the public launch. Every property gets a written marketing plan. You'll see it before we start.

5. The offer is the easy part. The deal is the hard part.

Accepting an offer feels like the finish line. It isn't. Subject removal, inspection, financing, the buyer's lender, the buyer's lawyer, the surveyor, the title insurer. All of these can come back with surprises. Our job is to make sure they don't, and when they do, to keep things moving without losing the deal.

6. Plan the move, not just the sale.

Completion, possession, moving day, where the kids sleep that night, what happens to the cat. The transaction is one piece of a much larger week of your life. We help plan that week, not just the closing.

The best seller's agent makes the whole thing feel boringly smooth.
